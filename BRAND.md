# Brand Guide — Full Kit Marketing

A quick reference for voice, naming, and visual identity. Source of truth is the
Full Kit brand identity system (see `source/FullKitMarketing_BrandKit.pptx.pdf`).

## Name

**Full Kit Marketing** is the trade name and wordmark — use it in the header, titles, OG, footer,
and schema. The legal entity is **Full Kit Marketing LLC** (Oregon), used where legally required
(footer, privacy policy, contracts). The founder is **Rachel Beck** — keep in bios, about, and schema.
The site lives at **fullkitmarketing.com**.

## Taglines & approved phrases

| Slot | Phrase | Where it goes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Headline / display** | Marketing that moves small business forward. | Hero `<h1>`, OG title/image, slogan |
| **Body promise** | Marketing to fuel your business to the next milestone, and beyond. | Sub-headlines, intros |
| **Header tagline** | Marketing that moves you forward | Brand subtitle under the wordmark (`.brand-sub`) |
| **Positioning** | A catalyst, not an agency that sells a menu. | What-we-are sections, pitch |
| **Specialty** | 0-1 marketing for growing service businesses | Meta descriptions, social bios |

**Guidelines**
- Full Kit is a **consultancy, not an agency**; a **catalyst, not a menu**. Keep that distinction sharp.
- Voice is **plain, confident, senior** — founder-to-founder, no corporate filler.
- Body copy may use **"we"** (the consultancy) and refer to **Rachel** by name where personal.

## Color (Full Kit brand identity system)

| Token | Hex | Role | Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `--plum` | `#3B1C32` | Primary · anchor | Hero/dark sections, footer, primary text, proposal covers |
| `--rust` | `#9B3D12` | Secondary · accent & energy | Primary CTAs, subheadings, link hover, accents on light |
| `--rust-br` | `#C75E30` | — | Brighter rust for accents on plum/dark backgrounds |
| `--steel` | `#7CA5B8` | Secondary · contrast & calm | Data callouts, badges, secondary support |
| `--sage` | `#627264` | Secondary · grounding | Section backgrounds, subtle dividers |
| `--bg` (white) | `#FFFFFF` | Neutral · canvas | Page canvas, card backgrounds |
| `--bg-alt` (warm white) | `#F8F4F0` | Neutral | Alt sections, print |

Color usage map: **plum on dark** for hero/CTAs/covers · **cream on light** for body/case studies ·
**rust accent** for pull quotes, subheadings, link hover · **steel** for data callouts, badges,
secondary buttons · **sage** for section backgrounds and dividers.

## Type

- **Display / headers:** EB Garamond (bold), serif. (`source` brand kit specifies EB Garamond Bold.)
- **Body / UI:** Avenir Regular. Self-hosted **Nunito Sans** is the web fallback when Avenir isn't
  installed (Avenir is not freely licensable for the web). CSS stack:
  `'Avenir Next', 'Avenir', 'Nunito Sans', system-ui, sans-serif`.
- Sizes (brand kit): H1 ~40px, H2 ~28px, H3 ~20px; body 16px, small 14px, eyebrow/caption 11px.

## Logo

A stacked wordmark **"Full Kit / Marketing"** in EB Garamond. The site mark/favicon
(`favicon.svg`) is an **ascending-bars + forward-arrow** emblem on a plum rounded square —
steel, cream, and rust bars rising left-to-right, signaling growth and forward motion.
