About Full Kit Marketing

Our name means something. Pro athletes don't take the field with a kit that’s missing pieces — and they wear a kit made specifically for their team. Full Kit works the same way: We assembles exactly what each client actually needs to reach their goals — from senior strategy down to execution — without the overhead of an agency or the gaps of a generalist freelancer.

After more than 13 years as an in-house marketer for everything from pre-launch startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, Rachel promoted herself to CEO of Full Kit Marketing in 2026. She believes that marketing works best when it’s like a pro’s kit: tailored, authentic, purposeful, and creative.

P.S. A kit, as you may know, is also a juvenile fox. Foxes are known for being clever, resourceful problem solvers (with a side of sauciness). We'd like to think that's how clients think of Full Kit Marketing, too.
