Services

Broad-spectrum. Never one-size-fits-all.

Every engagement is built around what your specific business actually needs. Full Kit draws on six core areas of expertise — what's included in any given engagement is determined by your goals, not a preset package.

Services are delivered through two models: ongoing monthly retainers for strategic partnership, and project-based engagements for discrete deliverables.

Core service areas

Six areas of expertise

01

Marketing strategy & planning

A full audit of where you stand — brand, positioning, audience, channels, and competitive landscape — followed by a strategic roadmap tied to your business objectives. This is the foundation of every Full Kit engagement.

02

Messaging & positioning

Defining your core value proposition, differentiating from competitors, and building a messaging framework that works across channels. Especially valuable for businesses launching, repositioning, or entering a new growth phase.

03

Content strategy & creation

Content production and distribution strategies designed to strengthen your connection with your audience, attract customers, and build brand loyalty.

04

Marketing operations & automation

Building and optimizing the marketing tech stack, setting up automation workflows, establishing reporting infrastructure, and creating the operational backbone that lets marketing scale — including implementing the AI tools relevant to your marketing: content generation, workflow automation, and customer insight. A core Full Kit differentiator, and among the highest-ROI services for small businesses.

05

Reporting & analytics

Setting up reporting frameworks that give you clear visibility into what's working — the metrics that actually matter, in dashboards leadership can use.

06

Custom brand activations

“Wow moments” that bring your business and brand to life.

Engagement models

Two ways to work together

Regardless of model, what's included is determined by your actual needs — not a package.

Ongoing partnership

Monthly retainer

$3,000–$4,000 / month

Ongoing strategic partnership: client and competitor research, strategy creation, planning, and guidance. For clients who need consistent marketing leadership and direction.

Best for growing businesses that need a senior marketing partner month after month.

Defined scope

Project engagement

$100 / hour

Defined-scope deliverables billed hourly — content calendar creation, tools training, drip email campaign setup, and similar discrete needs.

Best for a specific, bounded project where you know what you need delivered.

Where specialists are engaged for overflow or niche needs, that work is scoped into estimates upfront.
