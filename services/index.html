Questions

Common questions

What does Full Kit Marketing do?

Full Kit is a marketing consultancy for growing service businesses. We specialize in 0-1 marketing — getting businesses with little or no marketing infrastructure fully set up, strategically aligned, and operating with the systems and roadmap they need to scale.

Who does Full Kit work with?

Growing service-based businesses with 2–100 employees and 0–1 in-house marketers — hospitality, trades, professional services, SaaS, consulting and more. The common thread is real revenue and a real need for senior marketing thinking they haven't had access to before. E-commerce businesses are outside our target market.

How much does it cost?

Two models. Monthly retainers run $3,000–$4,000/month for ongoing strategic partnership. Project work is billed at $100/hour for discrete, defined-scope deliverables. Where specialists are engaged for overflow or niche needs, that's scoped into estimates upfront.

What are Full Kit's core service areas?

Six: marketing strategy & planning; messaging & positioning; content strategy & creation; marketing operations & automation (including AI tools); reporting & analytics; and custom brand activations. What's included in any engagement is determined by your actual needs, not a preset package.

Do you only work with one industry?

No — Full Kit is industry-agnostic within the service sector. The strategic and operational principles that make marketing work apply across hospitality, trades, professional services, SaaS, consulting and beyond. The unifying characteristic is your stage and the gap you need filled, not the type of service you provide.
