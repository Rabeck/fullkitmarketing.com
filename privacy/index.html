Privacy & Cookies

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Full Kit Marketing LLC (“Full Kit,” “we,” “us”) keeps this simple. We collect only what we need to reply to you, and we don't sell or share your information. This page explains what happens when you use fullkitmarketing.com.

The contact form

When you send a message through the contact form, you provide your name, email, and whatever you write in the message. We use those details only to read and reply to your enquiry. Form submissions are processed by Web3Forms, which delivers the message to us by email. We may also keep your message in our email so we can continue the conversation.

Spam protection

To keep out automated spam, the form uses a hidden honeypot field and may use Cloudflare Turnstile, a privacy-respecting alternative to CAPTCHA. Turnstile does not track you across websites.

Hosting & logs

The site is hosted on Cloudflare's network. Like any web host, Cloudflare processes basic technical information (such as your IP address and browser type) to serve the site securely and to protect against abuse. If privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics are enabled, they count page views without identifying you.

Cookies

This site does not set advertising or tracking cookies. Cloudflare may set strictly necessary cookies for security and to deliver the site. Fonts are self-hosted, so loading the site does not send requests to third-party font providers.

Your choices

  • You can email us anytime instead of using the form: rachel@fullkitmarketing.com.
  • You can ask us to delete the message and contact details you sent us.
  • You can use your browser settings to block or clear cookies.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information? Email rachel@fullkitmarketing.com.
